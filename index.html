Whiskey Cocktails

Classic Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 1/4 oz Maraschino cherry syrup
  • Orange quarter slice mixed in
  • Garnish with peel twist and cherry

Japanese Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2 oz Japanese whiskey
  • 3/4 oz Umeshu (plum wine)
  • Grated ginger (to try) or ginger beer?
  • Garnish with dried orange slice

Holiday Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 3/4 oz maple syrup
  • 2 dashes vanilla extract
  • Orange quarter slice and top w/ cinnamon and nutmeg
  • (Maybe an egg white?)

Whiskey Sour

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white

Manhattan

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes of bitters
  • Maraschino cherry

Experiment 0

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2-4 oz Hojicha
  • Bitters

Tequila Cocktails

Margarita

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz orange liquor
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Sprinkle salt
  • Garnish with a lime

Mezcal Margarita

  • 2 oz Mezcal
  • 1 oz orange liquor
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Sprinkle salt
  • Garnish with a lime

Tiajuana Mule

  • 2 oz tequila (or mezcal)
  • 4 to 6 oz ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Garnish with cucumber or lime

Paloma

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Grapefruit soda
  • Garnish with dried orange slice

Rum Cocktails

Daiquiri

  • 2 oz light rum
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • 1 oz lime juice

Vodka Cocktails

Martini

  • 3 oz Vodka or Gin
  • 3/4 oz dry vermouth
  • Optional: olive juice
  • Garnish with lemon peel

Moscow Mule

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 4 to 6 oz ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Garnish with cucumber or lime

Mixed Spirits Cocktails

Vesper

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • Garnish with a lemon peel

Rio Grande

  • 1 oz mezcal
  • 1 oz whiskey
  • Ginger
  • 3/4 oz honey
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • Garnish with lemon twist and grated cinnamon

Coffee Cocktails

Classic Espresso Martini

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1/2 oz Kahlua
  • 1 oz espresso
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • Garnish with whole coffee beans

White Russian

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1 oz Kahlua
  • 1 oz Baileys or eggnog
  • Garnish with grated or whole beans

Foggy Night

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1/2 oz Kahlua
  • 1 oz espresso or cold brew (depends on concentration level)
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes of vanilla extract
  • egg white
  • Garnish with grated chocolate

Coffee and Espresso

Espresso

  • ~21 g or ~3/4 fl oz

Double Espresso

  • ~42 g or ~1.5 fl oz

Americano

  • Espresso (21g) + 100g hot water or ~1:5 ratio

Lungo

  • Espresso (21g) + 21g hot water or ~1:1 ratio

Macchiato

Cortado

  • Espresso + milk; ~1:4 ratio

Flat White

  • Double Espresso + milk; ~1:4 ratio

Capuchino

  • Espresso + milk; ~1:6-7 ratio

Cafe Late

  • Espresso + milk; ~1:14 ratio

Milky Way
