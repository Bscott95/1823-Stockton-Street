Whiskey Cocktails

House Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • Orange wedge
  • 1 dash orange bitters
  • 2 dashes Angostora bitters
  • 1/3 oz rich turbenado syrup
  • 1/3 oz dash rich cherry syrup
  • Garnish with orange peel twist and cherry

Japanese Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2 oz Japanese whiskey
  • 3/4 oz Umeshu (plum wine)
  • Grated ginger (to try) or ginger beer?
  • Garnish with dried orange slice

Smoked Mapple Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 3/4 oz maple syrup
  • 1/8 oz vanilla extract
  • Orange quarter slice
  • Garnish with cinnamon and nutmeg

Whiskey Sour

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 1/4 rich turbenado syrup
  • 1 egg white

Manhattan

  • 2 oz Rye Whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes of Angostora bitters
  • Maraschino cherry

Experiment 0

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 2-4 oz Hojicha
  • Bitters

Tequila Cocktails

Margarita

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1/3 oz orange liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1/3 oz lime juice
  • 1/3 oz agave nectar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Garnish with a lime

Mezcal Margarita

  • 2 oz Mezcal
  • 1/3 oz orange liqueur
  • 2 dashes orange bitters
  • 1/3 oz lime juice
  • 1/3 oz agave nectar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Garnish with a lime

Tiajuana Mule

  • 2 oz tequila (or mezcal)
  • 4 to 6 oz ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Garnish with cucumber or lime

Paloma

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Grapefruit soda
  • Garnish with dried orange slice

Rum Cocktails

Daiquiri

  • 2 oz light rum
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • Garnish with a lime wheel

El Presidente

  • 1.5 oz light rum
  • 3/4 oz dry vermouth or white vermouth
  • 1/4 oz orange liqueur
  • 1/8 oz grenadine
  • Garnish with a orange peel and cherry

Vodka Cocktails

Martini

  • 2 oz Vodka or Gin
  • 1 oz dry vermouth
  • Optional: olive juice
  • Garnish with lemon peel

Moscow Mule

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 4 to 6 oz ginger beer
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Garnish with cucumber or lime

Mixed Spirits Cocktails

Vesper

  • 1.5 oz gin
  • 1 oz vodka
  • 1 oz dry vermouth
  • Garnish with a lemon peel

Rio Grande

  • 1 oz mezcal
  • 1 oz whiskey
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • Garnish with lemon twist and slice of ginger

The Martinez

  • 1.25 oz Gin
  • 1.25 oz sweet vermouth
  • 1/4 oz rich cherry syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostora bitters
  • 4 dashes Orange bitters
  • Garnish with lemon twist

White Lady

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz orange liqueur
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • eggwhite from small egg (3/4 oz)
  • Garnish with lemon twist

Coffee Cocktails

Classic Espresso Martini

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1 oz coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz espresso
  • 1/4 oz rich turbenado syrup
  • lemon peel oils
  • Garnish with whole coffee beans

White Russian

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1 oz Kahlua
  • 1 oz Baileys or eggnog
  • Garnish with grated or whole beans

Foggy Night

  • 1 oz vodka or rum
  • 1/2 oz Kahlua
  • 1 oz espresso or cold brew (depends on concentration level)
  • 1/4 oz rich turbenado syrup
  • 2 dashes of vanilla extract
  • egg white
  • Garnish with grated chocolate
